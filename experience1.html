Getting started

I first started skating in 2015, it was my favorite activity in the winter at that time.

After learning it for a year and half, I went to North Carolina where I continued my journey

Stage 2

In NC, I met my second coach Jamye, she sees my talent in figure skating and invited me to attend their sychronized skating team

I was selected to perform in the Christmas show in 2017, it was my first time to skate under the flashligh.

With hard practicing with my team mates, we won gold in a regional competition. Months later, we won the silver medal in the Northestern competition.


Stage 3

Later I came back to China in 2018 and continued my journey of sychronized skating and started training for single.


This was the first sychronized event I've attended in China, I was a substitute for our team because I was a bit little.

I am taking a level test for figure skating in 2019, and I got to level 8(max of 10) till 2023.

Practicing session in 2019, where our team trained for a whole month without rest. This is when I met our Chorographer from Chicago.

We won the bronze medal in the Chinese inter-club League right after our training session.

We won another silver medal in Chinese National Novice figure skating series, also in 2019.


Stage 4

Everything came to a stop since 2020 due to the global issue. But I still managed to keep practicing skating.

The Beijing Winter Games Open in 2021 is the only competition we've attended.

23-24 Chinese Inter-club League, we won silver medal in our first competition in senior group.

23-24 Chinese Figure skating Championships, this is the competition that awarded all our team members Master level of Perfessional Althlete.


24-25 Chinese Inter-club League, together with Sweet Dream senior team, we won the Silver medal.
