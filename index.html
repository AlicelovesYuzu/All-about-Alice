My Figure Skating Philosophy

Having skated for 10 years, figure skating is not just a sport for me; it's a form of art and self-expression. Through each routine, I strive to convey emotions and stories, captivating the audience with every movement on the ice.

My passion for ice skating taught me that perfection is not a destination, but a relentless pursuit of balance—between power and grace, between technical precision and emotional storytelling. That same pursuit is what drew me to AI and mathematics.
